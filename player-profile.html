Featured Players

Sachin Tendulkar

Known as the "God of Cricket," Sachin Tendulkar is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport. With over 30,000 international runs, he holds numerous records in both ODI and Test cricket.

Career Statistics:
  • Matches: 664
  • Runs: 34,357
  • Centuries: 100
Notable Achievements:
  • First player to score 100 international centuries
  • 2011 Cricket World Cup winner
  • Most runs in World Cup history

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is a modern-day cricket legend and one of the most successful batsmen across all formats. Known for his aggressive playing style and leadership, Kohli has set several records in the shortest span of time.

Career Statistics:
  • Matches: 500+
  • Runs: 23,000+
  • Centuries: 70+
Notable Achievements:
  • Fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 ODI runs
  • 2011 Cricket World Cup winner
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (multiple times)

Rahul Dravid

Rahul Dravid, also known as "The Wall," is celebrated for his solid technique and resilience. He is one of the few players to have scored over 13,000 runs in both ODI and Test cricket.

Career Statistics:
  • Matches: 508
  • Runs: 13,288
  • Centuries: 36
Notable Achievements:
  • One of the top five run-scorers in Test cricket history
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2004
  • 2003 Cricket World Cup runner-up

MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, often called "Captain Cool," is known for his calm demeanor and incredible finishing ability. He led India to several significant victories, including the 2007 T20 World Cup and the 2011 ODI World Cup.

Career Statistics:
  • Matches: 538
  • Runs: 17,266
  • Centuries: 10
Notable Achievements:
  • ICC T20 World Cup winner (2007)
  • ICC ODI World Cup winner (2011)
  • Most successful captain in IPL history

Shane Warne

Shane Warne is widely regarded as one of the greatest bowlers in cricket history. With his unique spin bowling style, he took over 700 Test wickets, earning acclaim as a cricketing legend.

Career Statistics:
  • Matches: 145
  • Wickets: 708
  • Best Bowling: 8/71
Notable Achievements:
  • Wisden Cricketer of the Year (multiple times)
  • 2003 Cricket World Cup winner
  • Inducted into the ICC Cricket Hall of Fame in 2009
