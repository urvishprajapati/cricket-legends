India vs Sri Lanka - 2011 Cricket World Cup Final

The 2011 Cricket World Cup Final was a historic match where India faced Sri Lanka in the iconic Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai. With Gautam Gambhir's crucial 97 and MS Dhoni's unbeaten 91, India chased down Sri Lanka’s challenging total. This victory marked India’s second World Cup win and was a moment of immense pride for Indian cricket fans.

England vs New Zealand - 2019 Cricket World Cup Final

The 2019 Cricket World Cup Final between England and New Zealand was one of the most dramatic matches in cricket history. The match ended in a tie, leading to a Super Over that also tied. England emerged victorious based on the boundary count rule, winning their first-ever World Cup. This final will always be remembered for its nail-biting finish and intense moments.
